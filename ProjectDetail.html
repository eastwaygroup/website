Featured Project — Industrial

APEX LOGISTICS HUB

Project Overview

The Apex Logistics Hub is a 1.2 million square foot industrial distribution facility located in the heart of Chicago's logistics corridor. Delivered on schedule and within budget, this landmark project required the coordination of over 400 trade workers across 18 months of continuous construction.

EASTWAY served as the general contractor and construction manager, overseeing all phases from groundbreaking through final commissioning. The facility features state-of-the-art loading dock infrastructure, automated sortation systems, and energy-efficient MEP installations.

Scope Highlights

  • check_circle Pre-construction planning, value engineering, and site coordination
  • check_circle Structural steel erection — 8,400 tons of fabricated steel
  • check_circle Tilt-up concrete panel installation across 6 dock bays
  • check_circle Full MEP systems integration including 2MW electrical service
  • check_circle Site civil work: roads, utilities, stormwater management
  • check_circle Trade scheduling, quality control, and milestone delivery
