Featured Project — Residential

RESIDENCE AT CACHET COUNTRY CLUB

Project Overview

The Residence at Cachet Country Club is a prestigious residential construction project located in the sought-after Cachet community of Markham, Ontario. EASTWAY was engaged to deliver a high-quality custom home build that reflects the refined character of the surrounding estate neighbourhood.

Situated within one of Markham's most established communities, this project demanded meticulous attention to detail across every phase — from foundation work and framing through to finishing and exterior landscaping integration. Our team coordinated all trades to ensure the highest standard of craftsmanship throughout.

Scope of Work

  • check_circle Site preparation, excavation, and foundation construction
  • check_circle Structural framing, roofing, and exterior envelope
  • check_circle Full mechanical, electrical, and plumbing rough-in and finish
  • check_circle Custom interior millwork, flooring, and high-end finish selections
  • check_circle Kitchen, bathroom, and living space build-out to owner specifications
  • check_circle Exterior finishing, driveway, and site restoration
