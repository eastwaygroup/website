OUR STORY

HISTORY OF EXCELLENCE

Established in 1988, EASTWAY is a well-established general contractor with 35 years of experience in residential and commercial construction across Canada. Our dedication to superior quality, attention to detail, and client satisfaction has grown us into a group of companies specialized in building supplies, millwork fabrication, hospitality renovation, subdivision, and high-rise construction — with a proven track record of successful projects valued from $1M to $50M.

Our Leader

EASTWAY's success is led by its founder Raymond Wan, a University of Toronto graduate who came to Canada from Hong Kong in 1982. With 35 years as a registered Professional Engineer in Ontario, Raymond brings extensive expertise across all aspects of construction — from sourcing materials to analyzing complex project requirements, meeting building codes, and ensuring every project is delivered on time and within budget.
